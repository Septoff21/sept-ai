# Hi, I'm Sept 👋

I build, break, and document things at the intersection of AI and software engineering. **Sept-AI** is my attempt to keep up with a field that reinvents itself every quarter — a personal navigator that collects what's worth reading, organizes what's worth trying, and remembers what I've learned.

## What you'll find here

- 🧭 **A navigator** — trending AI news, tools, models, and agents, refreshed every 6 hours from 40+ sources
- 👤 **A knowledge layer** — my [[Claude-Learning]] notes, [[Prompts]] library, and [[Workflows]]
- 🧪 **Experiments** — things I'm trying, shipping, or giving up on

## Why build this?

Reading RSS feeds one at a time is inefficient. Bookmarking tools in a browser dies silently. Notion dashboards rot. I wanted one place that:

1. **Updates itself** — no manual curation burden
2. **Lives in markdown** — owned, portable, greppable
3. **Has graph view** — because AI is a web of connected ideas
4. **Is public** — if I'm learning, someone else might benefit
